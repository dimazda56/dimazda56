Who is Guts?

"Berserk Guts" refers to a character named Guts from the Japanese manga and anime "Berserk." Guts is the main protagonist of this story. "Berserk" is created and written by Kentaro Miura. Guts initially was a mercenary known for his exceptional strength and combat skills. His life becomes complex and tragic after the events unfold in "Berserk," and his character undergoes many trials. "Berserk" is known for its dark themes and visual style, and the character of Guts is one of the most memorable figures in the world of manga and anime.
